Electrical Diagnostics

Scan Tools  ·  Board-Level Equipment  ·  OEM Wiring Procedures

Our Approach

Trace. Test. Confirm.

Electrical diagnosis starts with the right tools and the right information. Our technicians work from OEM software and OEM wiring diagrams — the same factory resources used to build and service each vehicle. We carry advanced scan tools, board-level equipment, and we follow manufacturer-specified test procedures from start to finish. We trace, test, and confirm before any repair recommendation is made.

We serve both independent shops and the general public. Shops bring us in when they need an extra set of hands on a complex electrical case — we function as a technical resource for the trade. Vehicle owners come to us directly when they need a thorough, documented diagnosis. Either way, the goal is the same: keep cars on the road and keep our community moving.

How we work: Every diagnosis is performed using OEM wiring diagrams and factory scan data. We document our findings clearly — what was tested, what was found, and what the recommended path forward is. No guesswork. No assumptions.

Services Included

Fault Code Diagnosis & Verification

We retrieve and verify diagnostic trouble codes using OEM-level scan tools, cross-referencing factory diagnostic flow charts and live data to confirm the root cause — not just the symptom.

Circuit Tracing & Continuity Testing

Using OEM wiring diagrams, we trace circuits from component to source, testing for opens, shorts, high resistance, and intermittent faults that don't always show up on a basic scan.

Parasitic Draw Testing

For battery drain issues, we perform systematic parasitic draw testing — isolating circuits and modules to identify what's pulling current when the vehicle should be at rest.

Module Communication Diagnostics

We test network communication across CAN, LIN, and other bus systems to identify modules that are offline, sending bad data, or affecting the behavior of other systems on the vehicle.

Charging & Starting System Analysis

Full evaluation of the charging and starting system — alternator output, battery condition, voltage drop across the system, and starter circuit performance under load.

Body Electrical & Accessory Circuits

Lighting, power accessories, HVAC controls, wipers, windows — we diagnose body electrical complaints using factory schematics and proper test equipment, not trial-and-error part swaps.

Custom Wiring & Repair Procedures

When wiring repair is required, we follow OEM repair procedures and use proper splicing, sealing, and connector techniques. Repairs are done to factory specification.
