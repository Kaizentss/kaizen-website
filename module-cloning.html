Service Overview

Seamless Module Replacement

Module cloning allows us to transfer the exact programming from your original control unit to a replacement module — no relearn issues, no VIN mismatch, no lost configurations. We specialize in board-level work and Borg econbox procedures for BCM, ECM, and TCM replacements.

Why cloning instead of replacement? A new replacement module requires VIN programming, key relearns, and often extensive reprogramming procedures. Cloning transfers your original module's data directly — the vehicle sees it as the original unit. Faster, more reliable, and preserving every configuration your vehicle had.

BCM

Body Control Module

Clone your BCM to a donor unit without losing door lock profiles, lighting configurations, or security system settings.

ECM

Engine Control Module

Full ECM data transfer including fuel trim adaptives, immobilizer data, and calibration files. No relearn required.

TCM

Transmission Control Module

TCM cloning preserves shift adaptives and transmission learned values — critical for smooth operation post-replacement.

BORG

Econbox Borg Work

Specialized cloning procedures for econbox-level vehicles with integrated or combined control modules. Full data integrity guaranteed.
